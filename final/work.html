SAMPLING OF MY WORK

GRAPHIC DESIGN
Design is my bread and butter. I love the challenge of solving a problem visually. There are always new trends to try and add your own twist to and I find great joy in exploring this realm of creativity.

In my projects, I look to solve the problem at hand first and then look to add creative flare. I like to collect all the should-be could-be information we want to communicate and work towards presenting three strong iterations.

Through all my design you will find thoughtful consideration to balance and the respect to text and graphical elements.
